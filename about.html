A Bank Owned by its Members

PanteeranKave Service Co-operative Bank (PSCB) was established in 1998 with the goal of providing a safe haven for the savings of local residents and a source of affordable credit for farmers and small business owners.

Unlike traditional banks, our profits are reinvested into our community and shared with our members. Every decision we make is guided by the welfare of the people of PanteeranKave and the surrounding regions.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and member-friendly co-operative bank in the state, leveraging technology to offer world-class banking experiences while remaining true to our rural roots and community values.

Our Mission

To empower our members through competitive financial products, promote thrift among the community, and provide swift, transparent assistance for the socio-economic development of the region.
